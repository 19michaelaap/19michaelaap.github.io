Hello! I'm Michaela Polley

I am a second-year graduate student in the mathematics department at Dartmouth College. My advisor is Sergi Elizalde. My research (so far) is in enumerative combinatorics. Currently, I think a lot about patterns in rectangulations.

Prior to attending Dartmouth, I completed my undergrad work at Carleton College in Northfield, MN, where I majored in mathematics and chemistry and minored in public policy. I also spent one year at the University of Klagenfurt doing research with Andrei Asinowski on a Fulbright grant.
